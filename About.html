“Our Story”

📜 Founding History

Founded : 1992

Location : Bengaluru, India

Founders’ Vision: :
The Artemisia Art Museum was established by visionary art historian Dr. Meera Srinivasan and philanthropist Rajan Kapoor, with a dream to create an inclusive space where ancient traditions and modern creativity coexist. They believed that art must be accessible, understandable, and immersive — not locked behind elitism or silence.

🎯 Mission Statement

“We aim to preserve and present meaningful human expression across centuries.”

We are committed to:

  • Bridging the gap between classical and contemporary Indian art
  • Supporting local and indigenous artists
  • Making art education and exploration available to all age groups
